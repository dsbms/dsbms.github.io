idea: "Software that designs targeted mass-spectrometry assays and in-silico spectral libraries for lipids."
lead: "Targeted, quantitative lipidomics needs carefully designed transition lists and spectral libraries. LipidCreator provides a complete framework to define fragments, compute their masses and export ready-to-use assays — lowering the barrier to reliable quantification."
concepts:
  - "Computes MS/MS fragment masses for over 60 lipid classes."
  - "Handles stable-isotope labeling and collision-energy optimization."
  - "Generates in-silico spectral libraries and integrates with Skyline."
findings:
  - "Assays validated both computationally and analytically."
  - "Scales to large targeted experiments for blood-plasma analysis."
  - "Applied to dissect lipid-signaling pathways in human platelets."
